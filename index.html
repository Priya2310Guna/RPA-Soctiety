About Us

The RPA Society, initiated by St. Joseph's Group of Institutions and led by Mrs. Janani Muralitharan, serves as a dynamic platform for students to explore and excel in Robotic Process Automation (RPA). This society is a collaborative effort involving two esteemed colleges, St. Joseph's Institute of Technology and St. Joseph's College of Engineering, each with its own dedicated Student Developer Champion (SDC) and core team.The RPA Society is designed to foster a community of like-minded students who are passionate about automation and its transformative potential in the tech industry. By bringing together students from different academic backgrounds, the society aims to create a diverse learning environment where members can share knowledge, ideas, and innovations.

Through a range of activities, including workshops, projects, and hackathons, the society provides students with hands-on experience in RPA. These activities are carefully curated to enhance their skills, from understanding the basics of automation to implementing complex workflows in real-world scenarios.

The core teams in each college play a pivotal role in guiding members, offering mentorship, and organizing events that challenge students to push the boundaries of what RPA can achieve. The society also emphasizes collaboration and teamwork, encouraging members to work on joint projects that address real-world problems.By participating in the RPA Society, students not only gain technical expertise but also develop leadership skills, problem-solving abilities, and a deep understanding of the ethical implications of automation. The society aims to prepare its members to be at the forefront of the RPA industry, equipped with the knowledge and experience needed to drive innovation in the field. In essence, the RPA Society is more than just a student group; it is a vibrant community that nurtures talent, fosters innovation, and prepares the next generation of RPA professionals to lead in an increasingly automated world.
